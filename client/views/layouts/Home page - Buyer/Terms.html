2. About FarmLink
FarmLink is a digital marketplace that connects farmers, buyers, and NGO coordinators. Our platform supports fair trade, transparent pricing, and efficient farm-to-market delivery.

3. User Responsibilities
• Farmers / NGOs – Create accurate crop listings, update stock availability, and ensure correct information.
• Buyers – Purchase verified products directly from farmers through the platform.
• FarmLink Team – Maintains the system, ensures data integrity, and promotes fair platform usage.

5. Product Listings & Transactions
• Prices and quantities shown are based on real-time data from farmers and verified by NGO coordinators.
• “Rescue Deals” (discounted near-expiry or oversupply produce) are automatically applied to reduce waste.
• FarmLink is not a direct seller or buyer—our role is to connect users within the marketplace.

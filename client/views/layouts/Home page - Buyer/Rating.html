What is Freshness Rating?

The Freshness Rating is a system that shows how recently a crop was harvested and how long it is expected to stay at its best quality. It displays a countdown (e.g., “Best within 2 days”, “Freshly harvested today”) based on the type of produce and its natural shelf life.

This helps buyers quickly understand:

  • How fresh the produce is
  • How soon it should be consumed or distributed
  • Which items need priority purchase

For farmers, it adds transparency and improves trust. For buyers, it ensures confidence that they are purchasing fresh, quality goods.

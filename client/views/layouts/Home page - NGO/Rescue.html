Rescue Alerts are notifications sent by the FarmLink system when
a farmer has surplus produce or crops that are nearing their
freshness limit. Instead of letting the products go to waste, the
app automatically marks them as discounted “rescue deals” and
alerts buyers, NGOs, or bulk purchasers who may want to
purchase them quickly.

This feature helps:
• Reduce food waste
• Support farmers by helping them sell products that might
otherwise spoil
• Provide buyers with lower-priced fresh produce
• Improve overall efficiency in the local food supply chain

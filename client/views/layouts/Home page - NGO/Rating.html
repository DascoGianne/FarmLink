The Freshness Rating is a system that shows how recently a crop
was harvested and how long it is expected to stay at its best
quality, it displays a countdown(eg.,"Best within 2 days",
"Freshly harvested today") based on the type of produce and its
natural shelf life.

This helps buyers quickly understand:
• How fresh the produce is
• How soon it should be consumed or distributed
• Which items need priority purchase

For farmers, it adds transparency and improves trust. For buyers,
it ensures confidence that they are purhasing fresh, quality
goods.
